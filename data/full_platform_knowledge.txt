# SSD ALIP Knowledge Corpus

## 1. Platform Overview
The SSD ALIP (AI-Powered Learning Intelligence Platform) is an advanced workforce learning and assessment platform tailored for SSD data professionals. It leverages artificial intelligence to provide personalized learning experiences, technical skill assessments, and comprehensive analytics, thereby modernizing workforce learning and development.

## 2. Business Purpose
The primary objective of SSD ALIP is to enhance the skills and competencies of data professionals within the organization. By utilizing AI-driven recommendations and assessments, the platform aims to streamline the learning process, ensure skill alignment with business needs, and foster a culture of continuous learning.

## 3. AI Features
- **AI-Powered Learning Recommendations**: Personalized course suggestions based on learner profiles and skill gaps.
- **AI-Generated Technical Skill Assessments**: Automated generation of assessment questions tailored to specific skills.
- **Learner Assessment History Tracking**: Comprehensive tracking of learner performance over time.
- **Workforce Learning Analytics**: Insights into learning trends, skill acquisition, and workforce readiness.
- **Personalized Learning Workflows**: Customized learning paths that adapt to individual progress and preferences.
- **Enterprise Skill Intelligence Dashboards**: Visual dashboards that provide an overview of skill levels across the organization.

## 4. Technical Architecture
The SSD ALIP is built on a modular architecture that separates the logic layer from the presentation layer. This architecture supports AI-powered recommendation workflows and an assessment engine, ensuring scalability and maintainability.

## 5. Technology Stack
- **Frontend**: Streamlit
- **Backend**: Python
- **AI/LLM**: OpenAI gpt-4.1-mini
- **Data Processing**: Pandas
- **Storage**: CSV and JSON persistence
- **Deployment**: Streamlit Community Cloud
